General Questions
What is TrueLink? ▼
TrueLink is a Universal Trust ID platform. Every person gets a unique verified ID (e.g. TL-4829-XK) that they can share on any platform — Instagram, OLX, WhatsApp, Fiverr — anywhere. It lets others verify who they are dealing with before sending money or hiring someone online.
Is TrueLink free? ▼
Yes! Creating a TrueLink ID is completely free. Basic verification (phone + email) is free. Government ID verification is also free during our launch period.
How is TrueLink different from LinkedIn or Aadhaar? ▼
LinkedIn is only for professionals. Aadhaar only works in India and has no trust score. TrueLink works for every person, on every platform, globally. It includes proof-based reviews, deal certificates, and a freelancer marketplace — all in one place.
Where can I use my TrueLink ID? ▼
Anywhere! Add it to your Instagram bio, OLX profile, WhatsApp status, business card, email signature, Fiverr profile — anywhere you interact with people online. Others can search your ID on TrueLink to verify you instantly.
Verification Questions
What documents are needed for verification? ▼
You can verify with any one of these: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Voter ID. The document is only used for verification — it is never shared with anyone.
Is my government ID safe? ▼
Yes. Your ID is encrypted and stored securely. We only use it to verify your identity — we never sell your data or share it with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.
How long does verification take? ▼
Phone and email verification is instant. Government ID verification takes up to 24 hours. Once verified, your profile shows a "✓ Government ID Verified" badge.
Review Questions
How do I leave a review? ▼
Go to Leave a Review. Search the person's TrueLink ID, give a star rating, write your experience, and upload proof (payment screenshot, chat history, or receipt). Our system verifies the proof automatically.
Why is proof required for reviews? ▼
To prevent fake reviews. Anyone can write anything — but proof shows it actually happened. This makes TrueLink reviews trustworthy unlike other platforms where fake reviews are common.
What if someone leaves a fake review on my profile? ▼
Our system blocks fake reviews automatically. If a fake review gets through, you can report it. We will investigate within 24 hours and remove it if found fake. The person who submitted the fake review will face account suspension.
Marketplace Questions
How is TrueLink Marketplace different from Fiverr? ▼
Fiverr takes 20% of every payment. TrueLink takes zero platform cut — you keep 100% of your earnings. We only charge a small fee (1-2%) for the payment witness service when used. Plus every freelancer is Government ID verified.
How do I list my service on the marketplace? ▼
Go to Marketplace and click "+ Post My Service". Fill in your TrueLink ID, service title, description, and starting price. Your listing goes live after verification within 24 hours.
Safety Questions
What is a TrueLink Deal Certificate? ▼
After every completed deal, both parties receive a TrueLink Deal Certificate with a unique ID. This certificate is legal evidence of the transaction and can be used in case of any future dispute.
What happens if someone scams me? ▼
Report the person on their profile page. Submit your proof (payment screenshot, chat history). Our team will review within 24 hours. A verified bad review will appear on their profile permanently, warning others. Repeat scammers get permanently banned.
